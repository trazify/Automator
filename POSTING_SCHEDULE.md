## 🧠 Strategy Explained

### Why Upload BEFORE Peak?

YouTube Shorts tests your video **30-90 minutes AFTER upload**, not instantly.

**Example:**
- Peak time: 8:00 AM
- Upload time: 7:30 AM
- Testing window: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM ✅ (Perfect!)

If you upload AT peak (8:00 AM), testing happens 8:30-9:30 AM (after peak).

## ⚠️ Critical Rules

### 1. Avoid Self-Competition
Don't upload multiple channels at same time. Current spacing:
- 7:30 AM - Luxury
- 10:00 AM - AI (2.5 hours gap)
- 5:30 PM - Anime (7.5 hours gap)

### 2. IP Clustering Prevention
YouTube may flag multiple uploads from same IP at same time. Our schedule naturally avoids this.

### 3. Algorithm Testing Window
Always upload 30-90 minutes BEFORE your target peak time.
